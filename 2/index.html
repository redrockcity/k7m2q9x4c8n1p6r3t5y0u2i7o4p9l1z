Downtown Campus

A historic schoolhouse becomes a school again.

Our new campus at 402 E. Blanco brings Crestmont into downtown Boerne. Built in 1910, the building once served as Boerne's first high school and later as city hall. For the 2026-27 school year, it returns to its original educational purpose.

Founding Families

Join the first downtown cohort.

Families who join us for the 2026-27 school year will be recognized as Founding Families of the Heart of Boerne campus, honoring their trust as we begin this new chapter downtown.

  • Recognition with gratitude Acknowledgment may include a future roster or display, a welcome gathering, and a note from school leadership.
  • Connection to a defining year This is a simple way to mark the families joining Crestmont at a meaningful moment in our story.
  • Recognition, not exclusivity The point is belonging and gratitude, not a special tier or discount program.
